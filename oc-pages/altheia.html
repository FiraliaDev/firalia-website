About

Altheia is the older sister of Cynthia, and twin sister of Athens. Prior to the Final Days, she worked as a researcher for the Words of Lahabrea.
Despite the age gap between them (around 6 years equivalent), she was always fond of her little sister, and was the most enabling of her antics out of the two older siblings. The sisters were always close, and when Cynthia set her heart on the spires of Amaurot, Altheia went with her.

In the end, Altheia was chosen as a sacrifice for Zodiark, and along with Hythlodaeus, never got a chance to say goodbye or break the news of their departure.
