- Screenshots are okay. Just ask and I'll strike a pose for you!

For Roleplay Partners

It's worth noting that I, the player, am greysexual/greyromantic, which is a fancy way of saying I fall under the asexual umbrella.
As a result, romantic scenarios can be difficult for me at times, but that doesn't mean I don't enjoy them. Far from it! It just means I might need a little extra guidance and patience. If you see me go quiet, don't be afraid to check in with me OOC. There's a good chance I'm just stuck in my head wondering what to do and need a little nudge, but it's good to check either way!

If you'd like to know more about how my sexuality impacts how I roleplay, don't be afraid to reach out privately.
Some research into aegosexuality and aegoromanticism is a great place to start!
